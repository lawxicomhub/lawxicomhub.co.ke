LAWXICOM CONSULTANT LTD

Lawxicom Consultant Ltd is a kenyan consulting company. began operations in January 2014 as a sole proprietorship before registering as a limited company in August 2020.
Our goal is to meet the demands of our clients by offering original, imaginative, workable, and high-quality solutions to business problems that are also
affordable, sociably desired, and environmentally sustainable.
